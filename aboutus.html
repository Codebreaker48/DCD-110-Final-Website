About Us

Bluegrass Burnouts is a Youtube Channel that was created by 6 friends who wanted to share their love for NASCAR. Though work and school got in the way, two of those six decided the project would continue. Now, each week of the NASCAR Season, you can join us to hear Zack and Cody talk about the sport that they love.

During the week, we post Race Reviews, Power Rankings, Paint Scheme Previews, and Race Previews. Each one focusing on our own opinions and what we actually thing about the product that NASCAR is producing. You will hear us talk about anything from attendance and horsepower, to rivalries and who is right, and much much more.
